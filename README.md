# STYLENOVA.AI
This application uses advanced AI technologies like, chatGPT and Leonardo.ai to offer personalized outfit recommendations based on your preferences and the latest fashion trends. With integrated Amazon shopping, you can directly find and purchase clothes and accessories from your recommended outfits without leaving the app. With our AI-driven outfit visualization feature, you can get a sneak peek of how each recommended outfit would look on you.
